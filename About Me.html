About Me

  My name is Sabrina Arce and I am currently in the eleventh grade. I am a 16 year old high school student, attending Miami Arts Studio 6-12. I am enrolled in the tech production magnet. I am a very creative and detail-oriented student. I am a drawing-loving 16 year old who was born and raised in Miami. My love for drawing and creating started when I was about seven or eight years old, when I moved to a different house. I would buy clay and make flowers and little people. I would also beg my mom let me re-vamp my things. I used to get old shirts and jeans and make purses out of it. When I would get bored of my regular clothing, I would cut my clothes and paint it to turn it into something new. To this day, I still do this but now I am more into just doodling and designing for others. When I was in elementary, I had an art teacher who never liked any of my work; because of her I became very insecure. I was very shy and scared to think outside the box because I didn't want to be judged. In middle school, I finally came to the realization that you can't always please everyone and that what others think of your art shouldn't affect you. I had to learn to be true to myself and make whatever type of art made me happy, even if it was weird. The weirdness is what made me who I am. I like to design and make things that anyone will be able to interpret regardless of where they are from and what language they speak. I have more confidence in my work now and have had my art chosen for the productions at school..

Graphic Designer

Being a graphic designer is the perfect career for me because I am very creative. Since I love to draw and come up with new ideas, designing comes easy to me. I  am always ready to take on a challenge and learn new methods for designing.

Marketing Manager

By being a graphic designer, I could also be a marketing manager because I would be exposed to new creative venues and ideas. I would also get to help different companies and organizations with the ways they advertise and get their messages across.

Business Owner

I've always wanted to be the owner of my own business because I want to be able to choose the way things should be run and designed. By being a business owner,  I would also be able to pursue my passion, get things done faster and communicate with clients. 

Photographer

I would like to be a photographer because I would like to share the way I see the world with others. I would also be able to experiment with different types of media.
