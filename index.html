BIO

Graphic Designer

Being a graphic designer is the perfect career for me because I am very creative. Since I love to draw and come up with new ideas, designing comes easy to me. I  am always ready to take on a challenge and learn new methods for designing.

Marketing Manager

By being a graphic designer, I could also be a marketing manager because I would be exposed to new creative venues and ideas. I would also get to help different companies and organizations with the ways they advertise and get their messages across.

Business Owner

I've always wanted to be the owner of my own business because I want to be able to choose the way things should be run and designed. By being a business owner,  I would also be able to pursue my passion, get things done faster and communicate with clients. 

Photographer

I would like to be a photographer because I would like to share the way I see the world with others. I would also be able to experiment with different types of media.
